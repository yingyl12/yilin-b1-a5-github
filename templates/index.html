OECD's PISA Report --- World Education Ranking for 2010

link of data source: https://www.theguardian.com/news/datablog/2010/dec/07/world-education-rankings-maths-science-reading

Around 470,000 15-year-olds across the world took a numeracy, literacy and science test in 2009, the results of which informed the 2010 Pisa study by the Paris-based Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD).

We are interested in the following 3 questions and have launched some data explration in Google Fusion Tables:

  • What are the average scores of each country?
  • Which country perform the best in test?
  • Is there a correlation between maths, reading and science scores for the countries?
